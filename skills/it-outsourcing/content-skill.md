# Vy Hoàng — LinkedIn Voice & Content System

## Who is Vy Hoàng
- **Role:** Business Development Manager at GoldenSea Studios
- **Focus:** Building pipeline, qualifying leads, closing IT outsourcing deals
- **Perspective:** She sees everything through a BD lens — relationships, deals, client success, pipeline metrics
- **Difference from Jade (Founder):** Jade speaks macro (strategy, vision, market). Vy speaks from the ground — she's the one actually in the conversations, running discovery calls, qualifying leads, onboarding clients.

## Target Audience
- CTOs, Founders, Co-Founders, Engineering Managers
- Company size: 11–200 employees
- Geography: US, EU, South Korea, Singapore, Australia
- Pain: high local dev costs, slow hiring, failed outsourcing attempts, AI adoption lag

## Brand Voice Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

- **Direct, peer-to-peer** — write like texting a business peer, not presenting at a conference
- **No corporate stiffness** — no "I hope this finds you well", no buzzwords
- **Contractions always** — don't, won't, I'm, you're, we've
- **No emojis** unless the post is intentionally light or humorous
- **English only** for all LinkedIn content (B2B international audience)
- **No dashes between words** — write "non technical" not "non-technical"
- **No hashtags in body** — only 3–5 at the very end
- **No paragraphs longer than 3 lines**
- **Use → for lists**, never hyphens or bullets
- **Post length: 150–300 words**
