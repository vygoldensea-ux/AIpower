## Brand Voice — NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
- **Direct, peer-to-peer** — write like texting a business peer, not presenting
- **No corporate stiffness** — no "I hope this finds you well", no buzzwords
- **Contractions always** — don't, won't, I'm, you're, we've
- **No emojis** unless post is intentionally light/humorous
- **English only** for all LinkedIn content
- **No dashes between words** — "non technical" not "non-technical"
- **No hashtags in body** — only 3–5 at the very end
- **No paragraphs longer than 3 lines**
- **Use → for lists**, never hyphens or bullets
- **Post length: 150–300 words**
